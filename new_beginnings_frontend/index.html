💡 Seller Tips

1
Price it right

Research similar properties in your area before setting the price. Competitive pricing attracts more enquiries.

2
Use high-quality images

Listings with 5+ good photos get 3x more views. Natural lighting and wide-angle shots work best.

3
Write a detailed description

Mention unique features, nearby landmarks, and renovation history to stand out from similar properties.

4
Keep documents ready

Have title deed, EC, and occupancy certificate handy. Buyers who verify documents convert faster.

5
Respond quickly

Replying to enquiries within 1 hour increases your chance of closing a deal by 60%.
